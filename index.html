About Me

My Journey

Hi, I’m Jessica. My career began in software development, where I’ve had the opportunity to work on enterprise applications and backend systems using technologies like Java, Groovy, Grails, Scala, Python, GitLab, and Linux-based tools. Working within Agile teams has helped me develop strong problem-solving, collaboration, and troubleshooting skills while contributing to business-critical applications.

As my experience has grown, so has my interest in the infrastructure that powers modern applications. Understanding how systems are deployed, secured, monitored, and maintained has led me to focus on cloud computing, automation, DevOps practices, and platform engineering. I enjoy seeing the full picture — from application development to the infrastructure that supports it.

What I’m Working Toward

My goal is to continue building expertise in cloud technologies, automation, and infrastructure while leveraging my software development background to create reliable, scalable, and secure solutions.

I believe some of the best engineers understand both development and operations. Having experience on the software side has given me a strong appreciation for automation, system reliability, and building platforms that help teams move faster and more efficiently.

  • ⚙️ Deepening skills in DevOps & Platform Engineering — CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform

A Project With Purpose

One of my current builds is CysterKeto — a health tracking platform inspired by my own experience with PCOS. I created the project not only to strengthen my technical skills in cloud infrastructure and backend development, but also to build something meaningful for other women navigating similar challenges. It’s where my interest in DevOps and cloud-native architecture meets something I genuinely care about.
